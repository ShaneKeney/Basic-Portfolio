About Me

Shane Keney was born February 20th, 1994 and raised in Suffield, CT. He graduated from Suffield High school in 2012 prepared to attend the University of Connecticut. He later graduated from Uconn with a major in Computer Science and a minor in Mathematics. He now lives in Enfield, CT with his wife Erica Keney and has one child named Emerson. Together, Erica and Shane have two dogs: a Leonberger and Austrailian Shephard, and two cats.


He accepted a job out of college as a Software Developer for windows desktop deployment working specifically on calibration software for linear and angular displacement transducers. He later came accross an opprotunity to work as a Lead Mobile Developer for Spikeball Inc. where he has worked since June 2019. Shane enrolled in the Uconn Coding Bootcamp in November to further his current skillset and learn additional skills that would aid him in Full Stack Web Development as it pertains to his work at Spikeball.
